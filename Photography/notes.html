Camera :: Canon 4000D
Notes

Mostly shot in Av (aperture priority) and M Manual on low ISO, I adjust the ISO manually when the light gets complicated.

The 18-55mm handles most of what I shoot day to day. The 75-300mm comes out for anything that needs reach; windmills, wildlife, anything across a field.

The wide-angle and telephoto converters screw onto the front via the 55mm filter thread. Not optically perfect but light and they travel well.
